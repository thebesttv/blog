#+title: URI

- [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Uniform_Resource_Identifier][Uniform Resource Identifier]]
- [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/URL][Uniform Resource Locator]]

A URN may be compared to a person's name, while a URL may be compared to their
street address.  In other words, a URN identifies an item and a URL provides a
method for finding it.
